## Ingredients

- half an onion, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp minced ginger
- 2 cups cauliflower florets
- 1 sweet potato, peeled and diced
- 1 can tomatoes
- 1 can coconut milk
- 1 cup vegetable broth
- 1 can chickpeas
- 1 tbsp garam masala
- 1 tbsp curry powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 cups spinach

## Steps

- Saut√© the onions, garlic, and fresh ginger for 7 minutes.
- Transfer the onion mix to a slow cooker, and add remaining ingredients except spinach.
- Cook on low for 6 hours.
- Before serving, stir in the spinach, and heat for five more minutes
- Serve with coconut rice
